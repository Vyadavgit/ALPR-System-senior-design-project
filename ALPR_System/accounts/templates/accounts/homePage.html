About Us

This ALPR project is made with the collaboration of 5 students from the University of Texas at Arlington as a part of the Senior Design program. Upon completion, the team will continue to work on the project depending on the scopes and potential.

About the project

ALPR system is an Automated License Plate Recognition technology which will help all the household and business owner to easy access to open their gate and door by scanning their licence plate number. Moreover, ALPR also provides the users with an efficient and easy way to manage their user profiles.

Services

We provide Automated License Plate Recognition technology for your business as well as your personal household

Automated Scan

Our System can automatically scan a vehicle and provide access to the vehicles by checking their eligibility.

Add Vehicles

The application provides the user with an option to add their vehicles to the system such that the car can be allowed access upon scanning.

Available Parking Space

The system can notify the users about the available parking spaces such that the user can efficiently park their car.
